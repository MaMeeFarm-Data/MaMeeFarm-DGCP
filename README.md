## The Four Layers of the Protocol

### **1. Proof of Life (PoLIFE)**
Records from real days, real weather, real animals, real conditions.
It answers the question: *‚ÄúWhat actually happened today?‚Äù*

### **2. Proof of Labor (PoLABOR)**

chores, repairs, feeding, cleaning, moving, documenting.
It answers: *‚ÄúWhat work was actually done?‚Äù*

### **3. Proof of Human Work (PoHW)**
Where life and labor meet.
PoHW verifies that the data came from a human being
doing real work in a real environment.
This is the heart of the protocol.

### **4. DGCP ‚Äì Duck Governance & Continuous Proof**
The top layer.
It defines how the records are used, protected, stored,
and kept honest over time.
It is the rulebook of this entire system.

Together, these four layers create a way to record real-life data  
that cannot be replicated, simulated, or rewritten.
