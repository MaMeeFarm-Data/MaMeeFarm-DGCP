# DGCP™ Labor Ethics Code

DGCP™ protects the dignity of real human labor.

## 1. Dignity of Work
Every recorded task — no matter how small — is treated as valuable human labor.

## 2. No Exploitation of Data Labor
- No external party may extract economic value without permission.
- Workers control their own data assets.

## 3. Transparency by Design
- All usage of data must be explainable.
- No hidden algorithms or opaque processing.

## 4. Fair Recognition
Human workers must be acknowledged as:
- originators  
- primary rights holders  
- custodians of meaning  

## 5. No Forced Participation
Data labor must always be voluntary.
