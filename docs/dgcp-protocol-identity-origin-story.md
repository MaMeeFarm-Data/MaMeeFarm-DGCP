## 2. The Beginning: A Farm With Almost Nothing  
MaMeeFarm™ began in rural northern Thailand with:

- no institutional funding  
- no electricity grid  
- no internet infrastructure  
- no water system  
- no support network  
- no access to loans  
- only 7 ducks, 10 dogs, and a mother (MaMee) trying to survive  

The farm relied on a single failing smartphone, solar power that often ran out before midnight,  
and food gathered from the land—papayas, vegetables, and eggs laid one by one.

This was not a tech startup.  
It was survival.
